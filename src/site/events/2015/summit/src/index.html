About Dart Summit
Two days of sharing insight and experience building and deploying apps with Dart. Learn about the platform from Dart's founders and engineers, and connect with your fellow Dartisans.

Summit presentations will focus on real-world experiences, future platform features, how to optimize Dart apps, and more. We'll also have breakouts and panels for topics that Dartisans want to discuss.

Join us in San Francisco or via the live video streams.
